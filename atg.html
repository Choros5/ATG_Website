Who We Are

ATG is an innovative startup pioneering AI solutions, with a strong focus on Computer Vision. We’ve built groundbreaking projects like SafeLab AI, an Early Diagnosis System, an AI-Powered Traffic Light System, and an Online Teaching Platform, pushing the boundaries of technology to solve real-world challenges. Beyond innovation, ATG is committed to empowering the next generation of tech leaders. Through our MKU Coding Academy, we’ve provided free AI and coding training to over 50 students at Mount Kenya University (MKU), fostering technological growth and innovation.
